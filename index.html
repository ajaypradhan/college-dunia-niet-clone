NIET Noida Admission

Candidates seeking admission to any of the courses offered by NIET Greater Noida can do so either through UPSEE counseling or by appearing for the entrance test conducted by the institute.

Candidates applying through the UPSEE entrance exam can register through online mode only.

The college takes admission from 2 sources:

  • UPSEE Counseling: The student needs to appear for the UPSEE exam and appear in online counseling. There he/she needs to opt NIET as the preferred choice in any of the branches we offer.
  • Direct Admission: The college has its own admission test which comprises of
    • Objective type Aptitude / English test
    • Objective type Math test for B.Tech. applications (Students applying for Biotech branch are exempted from Math test)
    • Personal Interview

After this, your application and test scores are evaluated to ensure that they meet the eligibility criteria set by AKTU. An admission committee constituted of senior management and faculty members specializing in the field of study review all your application materials. You receive a letter from the Registrar Office, notifying you of your admission status.

Application Procedure
  • Candidates applying through UPSEE can apply by visiting the AKTU website and filling the online application form.
  • The candidate must opt for NIET as the preferred choice of the institute while filling the application form.
  • Application fee payment must be done online via credit/ debit card or net-banking. No other mode of payment is allowed.
  • Candidates should keep the printout of the confirmation page for record. Candidates are also advised to note down the application form number for any future reference
